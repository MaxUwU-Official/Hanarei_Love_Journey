Deadly Desires

About

Deadly desire is a SECOND mod of this project.

This Mod takes place in the year of 1995 and 2035.

1995s Mode

The protagonist is Erika Hayashi, mother of Amaya Hayashi.

Ayano Aishi

Ayano Aishi is the protagonist of Yandere Simulator. She is known as "Yandere-chan" to players of the game, and has been given the cute nickname "Yan-chan" by her classmates.


Ayano attends a post-highschool academy called "Akademi." On the first day of her second year at Akademi, Ayano came into physical contact with a young man named Taro Yamada. In that moment, she gained the ability to experience everything that had been missing from her life; she felt complete, she felt fulfilled, and she felt powerful emotions.


Without a moment's hesitation, Ayano know exactly what she has to do. Eliminated her rivals as soon as possible, by any means necessary, no matter what the cost.

Midu Akotaka

Midu Akotaka is a kind-hearted girl with a deep love for plants, nature, and all things green. She spends most of her time in the Gardening Club, tending flowers and teaching others how to care for them.


She’s the leader of the Gardening Club and one of the top biology students at Akademi, often seen carrying books about plant life or experimenting with new ways to help things grow.


Midu dreams of becoming a florist and helping her family’s flower shop bloom into something bigger - a place where people can reconnect with nature and find peace in greenery. She is hoping to confess her feelings before the week is over.

Sheino Kaisho

Sheino Kaisho is the charismatic and effortlessly talented leader of the Drama Club, admired by both students and teachers alike for her stage presence and natural charm. She’s the kind of person who can make everyone around her feel important, and she’s built her popularity on that warmth. However, beneath her friendly exterior lies a mind that is always calculating, always aware of how she can use every piece of information to her advantage.


Sheino doesn’t need to bully or intimidate her classmates like some of the other students at Akademi. Instead, she has a more subtle weapon: gossip. She’s a keen listener and knows exactly who to talk to and when, always collecting tidbits of information. If someone tells her a secret or shares some juicy gossip, Sheino won’t hesitate to use it to climb higher in the social hierarchy or to subtly turn the tide in her favor.


She might not start drama herself, but she’s always ready to fan the flames when it serves her interests. She’ll act like a confidant, all while positioning herself in a way that guarantees she comes out on top. In Sheino's world, the Drama Club is her stage—and everyone else is just an actor in her well-scripted play.

Mono Tenso

Mono Tenso is the second leader of the Gaming Club, known for her energetic, larger-than-life personality and her unwavering passion for gaming. With her fierce competitive streak and love for all things digital, she’s often the first one to jump into a new game, always pushing herself and others to level up.


Her energy is contagious — she inspires those around her to work harder and play smarter, making her a beloved figure among her fellow club members. While Mono is known for her skills and drive, she never forgets the importance of having fun, making sure everyone enjoys the game, no matter their skill level.


Her confidence and gaming prowess have earned her quite a following, with some boys trailing behind her, hoping to catch her attention or impress her. But Mono’s focus remains on one thing: proving she’s the best gamer at Akademi, and she’s not about to let anything or anyone distract her from that goal.

Puro Sawago

Puro Sawago is a quiet and introspective member of the Art Club, often seen with her sketchbook in hand, lost in her own world. Shy and socially awkward, she tends to avoid drawing attention to herself, blending into the background. Despite her reserved nature, Puro’s passion for art runs deep, though her works often leave others feeling uncertain. Her drawings lack clear themes, sometimes appearing unsettling or cryptic, reflecting the turmoil and confusion she feels inside.


A perfectionist at heart, Puro struggles under the pressure of expectations, leading her to frequently shift between different ideas and projects. She can never seem to settle on a single subject long enough to finish a piece, constantly second-guessing her work. Her timidity only amplifies this, making it even harder for her to express herself confidently, especially in a group setting.


Though she’s shy and keeps to herself, there’s a depth to Puro that those who get close to her can see - a quiet brilliance that’s often hidden behind her uncertainty. Her artistic journey may be a struggle, but it’s also a search for meaning and a way to express emotions she’s unable to articulate with words. Her withdrawn demeanor and socially awkward tendencies often leave others to wonder what lies beneath the surface, but to Puro, it’s all part of the process. In the end, her art is her own reflection, complex and unpredictable, much like herself.

Shonu Amoka

Shonu Amoka is one of the most popular and well-liked students at Akademi, known for her bubbly and infectious personality that lights up any room she enters. Always wearing a bright smile and exuding warmth, Shonu has an uncanny ability to make people feel at ease, even on their worst days. Her energy is so contagious that it’s impossible not to feel a little lighter when she’s around.


Whether it’s through her cheerful greetings, her spontaneous bursts of laughter, or her ever-positive outlook, Shonu is a natural source of joy. Her presence can turn even the dullest moments into something fun, and it’s no surprise that many students are drawn to her. Her lively spirit has earned her a loyal following, with some classmates constantly hanging around, eager to bask in her positivity.


But behind her endlessly cheerful exterior, there’s more to Shonu than just the girl with a bright smile. Despite her outward confidence. Her kindness and optimism make her a beloved figure at Akademi, but those who are closest to Shonu know that it’s not just her infectious energy that draws people in - it’s her genuine care and warmth that makes her a true friend to all.

Takagi Masami

Takagi Masami is a refined and graceful member of the Cooking Club, known for her gentle, ladylike demeanor that leaves a lasting impression on everyone she meets. With her quiet elegance and soft-spoken nature.


In the kitchen, however, Masami’s talent truly shines. Her cooking and baking skills are nothing short of extraordinary, allowing her to effortlessly create dishes that leave everyone in awe. Whether it’s a delicate pastry or a savory entrée, Masami’s attention to detail and precision in the kitchen set her apart from her clubmates. Her mastery of flavors and techniques earns her the admiration of both her fellow Cooking Club members and the students who are lucky enough to taste her creations.


While she may not seek the spotlight, Masami’s quiet competence speaks volumes. Her food is not just nourishment, but an experience - one that is crafted with love, care, and an unmatched level of skill. Though others often look to her for guidance, Masami remains humble, never boasting about her talents but letting her dishes speak for themselves. Her gentle nature and culinary prowess have earned her respect across the school, and Masami’s reputation as a gifted chef continues to grows.

Teika Yanshiwana

Teika Yanshiwana is Akademi’s top student, known not only for her exceptional intellect but for her relentless pursuit of perfection. On the surface, she’s a model of academic excellence-her name at the top of every leaderboard, her answers always precise. But beneath this polished exterior lies a constant, nagging thought: "I am not good enough."


It’s this inner drive that pushes Teika beyond what anyone else considers reasonable. When a teacher dismisses a subject as irrelevant, Teika dives deeper into it-compelled by a belief that even the smallest gap in her knowledge could be the one thing standing between her and true mastery. She can’t afford to leave any stone unturned, no matter how inconsequential it might seem to others. For her, there’s no such thing as "enough"-there’s only more to learn, more to achieve, more to prove. Her obsession with knowledge leads her to spend countless hours in the library, long after the sun has set and others have gone home. Surrounded by towering shelves of books, Teika finds solace in the quiet rustle of pages turning, the flickering of overhead lights, and the endless sea of information waiting to be absorbed. Here, she can control her surroundings, immerse herself in her studies, and escape the uncertainty that often shadows her own self-worth.


Despite her achievements, Teika’s humility is palpable. She doesn’t see herself as "special," but as someone who is always becoming-a student forever, even when she’s at the top. In her mind, knowledge is the only thing she can truly perfect, the only thing that can quiet that persistent voice of doubt. While others may admire her brilliance, Teika is haunted by the feeling that she’s never quite done enough. Her academic excellence is her way of silencing that voice, if only for a moment. Teika doesn’t strive for recognition, she strives for peace. Not the kind of peace that comes from accolades, but the kind that comes from knowing, with certainty, that she’s done everything she can to be the best.

Role: Top Student


Status: Rival


Personality: "Dunning-Kruger Effect" and "Intellectual humility"


Appearance: Week 7


Weakness: False Accusations

Mio Dashokono

Mio Dashokono is a member of the Delinquents, feared and respected in equal measure across Akademi. With a short temper and a reputation for settling disputes with her fists rather than words, Mio thrives on confrontation. Where others talk things out, she acts - swiftly and without hesitation.


Her abrasive attitude and unpredictable behavior make her unwelcome in most places on campus. Teachers keep their distance, and many students avoid crossing her path altogether. Whispers follow her wherever she goes - rumors of fights behind the gym, vandalism, and "activities" that no one dares to confirm. Whether the stories are true or not, Mio doesn’t care. In fact, she seems to enjoy the fear her presence inspires.


Despite her rough edges, there’s something compelling about her. Beneath the scowl and the cigarette smoke is a girl who plays by her own rules, never pretending to be someone she’s not. She doesn’t ask for acceptance - and she certainly doesn’t need it. Those few who earn her respect find a fiercely loyal ally, someone who won’t hesitate to stand up for them no matter the cost.

Yuri Kamonsho

Yuri Kamonsho is a high-ranking member of the Student Council, known across Akademi for her calm, commanding presence and unwavering commitment to order. Always composed and perfectly polished, Yuri carries herself with the kind of discipline and formality that turns heads - and silences rooms. To most students, she comes across as cold, distant, and almost robotic in the way she enforces school rules with unwavering precision. She’s not one for small talk or emotional displays. When Yuri speaks, it’s with clarity, purpose, and a tone that leaves little room for argument. She is strict to the letter, often seen patrolling hallways, mediating conflicts, and keeping a close eye on those who might disrupt the peace. Many students find her intimidating, and few dare to challenge her authority - those who do rarely make the same mistake twice.


But beneath that hard, diplomatic exterior is a side of Yuri few ever see. She may not show it outwardly, but her actions reveal her deep concern for the well-being of others. Every rule she enforces, every warning she issues, is rooted in a desire to protect her fellow students. Whether it’s stepping in to de-escalate a brewing fight or quietly checking in on someone skipping class, Yuri never overlooks a potential threat to a student’s safety - even if her methods seem impersonal. Her sense of justice is not fueled by ego or a desire for control, but by a sincere belief that structure creates security. Order, in her eyes, isn’t about dominance - it’s about care. Even if no one thanks her for it, even if she's misunderstood, Yuri is fine with being seen as the “cold enforcer,” as long as it means others can walk the halls without fear.


In rare moments, those closest to her catch glimpses of the warmth buried deep within - a subtle gesture of kindness, a quiet word of encouragement, or a soft look that betrays her true feelings. She may not be quick to open up, but Yuri Kamonsho is far more than the mask she wears. She is precise, principled, and unwavering in her mission to serve and protect - not just the rules, but the people behind them.

Emi Akiyama

Emi Akiyama is one half of Akademi’s most inseparable friendship duo, always seen by the side of her best friend, Midu Akotaka. Bright, bubbly, and endlessly outgoing, Emi is the kind of girl who lights up a room the moment she walks in. She talks to everyone, makes friends easily, and brings a natural warmth wherever she goes - even if she can sometimes be a little too curious for her own good. Her extroverted personality makes her easy to approach, and many students rely on her for advice, comfort, or just a cheerful presence during stressful days. Loyal to a fault, Emi is especially protective of Midu, and it's rare to see one without the other. They’ve been close since Midu’s first year, and their bond has only grown stronger with time.


While Emi seems like someone who’s always been this way, her past tells a different story. Few know that during middle school, she was extremely introverted - shy, withdrawn, and reluctant to open up to anyone. Something shifted during that time, though she never talks about it in detail. Whatever happened changed her, and now she lives each day determined to embrace connection rather than avoid it.


Despite her love for gardening, Emi has never joined the Gardening Club. When asked, she simply shrugs and says, "It's just a hobby, not my future." But some wonder if there’s more to that answer - something she chooses not to explain. Though she keeps some pieces of herself guarded, Emi remains a beloved presence at Akademi. She's the friend who sticks by you, the voice that lifts you up, and the smile that reminds you not to take life too seriously - even when she carries more than she lets on.

Kaede Hayashi

There are cruel people in the world. Then there are monsters who love cruelty - who find art in agony and music in the final scream of a dying rival. Kaede Hayashi was one of those people. She was the very first. The origin of a cursed bloodline that would carry her name for generations. History forgets her face, but never her legacy. Cold, obsessive, and psychopathic in both mind and soul, Kaede believed that love was a war - and one that must leave no survivors. She didn’t see rivals. She saw trespassers. And trespassers didn’t deserve mercy - only pain. Kaede Hayashi was not content to "win" her Senpai. She wanted him to look at her, and only her, with eyes that never blinked elsewhere. Possession was not enough. There had to be elimination. Silence. Obedience. Fear. And once she had him, there had to be nothing left of the others - no memory, no whisper, no burial. It wasn’t just love. It was domination.


From this obsession, she carved out a rule. One that would haunt every Hayashi after her like a blood-bound law:
"If you are born a Hayashi, You must win your Senpai. And you must be the only one left standing."

This was not advice. It was survival. A tradition soaked in violence and bound by blood. If a Hayashi failed to follow the rule, they would face a terrible fate: Corruption.

Once it began, the soul would rot from within - turning the Hayashi into something less than human, twisted by obsession and hatred, forever stained. But Kaede added one more layer to her cruel legacy, a failsafe born from paranoia:

- A corrupted Hayashi has only one chance to flee.
- If seen with a non-corrupted member,
- that member must eliminate them - slowly and painfully.
- Failing to do so will result in their own corruption.

This was Kaede’s gift. A curse designed to protect the bloodline by destroying the weak. She had no mercy in her bones. Her pleasure came from screams, from psychological destruction, from watching her victims beg until they could no longer make sound. Violence wasn't a means to an end - it was the end. No photographs remain. No gravesite is marked. Her name is never spoken outside the family.
And yet, her shadow walks behind every Hayashi girl or boy, whispering the same vow:
"Win. Eliminate. Or be erased."


Some say Kaede vanished, that she simply walked away once her work was done. Others believe her blood still exists in hidden corridors - alive and waiting, watching her descendants from the dark.Whatever the truth is, one thing remains certain: She was the most dangerous Hayashi that ever lived.
